More Than Bookkeepers — We're Your Partners

Jim and Rebecca Spaak have been self-employed since 1999. They built businesses from the ground up, made the mistakes that come with that, and learned every hard lesson along the way.

That's what they bring to every client relationship: not just technical bookkeeping skill, but the perspective of someone who's sat in your chair — who's worried about payroll, wondered about cash flow, and made decisions with incomplete information.

Streamline Pros was built on the belief that small business owners deserve the same quality of financial partnership that large companies take for granted. When you work with us, you're not a transaction — you're a relationship.
