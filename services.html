Service 01

New Business Consulting

Starting a business is exciting — and overwhelming. We love learning about your vision while sharing the financial expertise that helps you build it on a solid foundation from day one.

We work alongside you through the critical early stage: setting up your chart of accounts, establishing clean reporting habits, and making sure your QuickBooks environment grows with your business rather than against it.

QuickBooks Online setup and configuration tailored to your industry

Chart of accounts and category structure built for your business model

Financial goal-setting and cash flow forecasting for early-stage growth

Ongoing consulting as your business evolves and scales

Schedule a Free Consultation
Service 02

Prior Year Rebuilds

Inaccurate books don't just cause stress — they create real financial risk. Messy records lead to missed deductions, tax penalties, and decisions made on faulty data. A complete rebuild fixes all of that.

If your books aren't accurate, a "do over" can save you from significant tax liability and set a clean foundation going forward. We methodically reconstruct your financials, often uncovering savings in the process.

Complete audit and reconstruction of historical financial records

Identification of missed deductions and tax exposure before filing

Clean handoff to your CPA or tax preparer with organized documentation

New workflow setup to prevent the same issues from recurring

Get Your Books Cleaned Up
Service 03

Project Tracking

For contractors, builders, and service businesses, knowing your per-job profitability is the difference between sustainable growth and working hard to go nowhere.

We configure QuickBooks to generate a Profit and Loss for each client job, so you always know exactly where the money is going — and which projects are actually worth taking on.

Job-level P&L reporting for every client project or contract

Labor and material cost allocation at the project level

Budget vs. actual comparisons to identify scope creep early

Custom reporting dashboards so you always know your numbers

Track Your Projects
Service 04

Gusto Payroll

As a certified Gusto Silver Partner, we set up and manage one of the best small business payroll platforms on the market. Gusto handles your payroll tax filings, direct deposits, and employee benefits in one clean interface.

Paired with QuickBooks, the integration is seamless — every payroll run syncs directly to your books, eliminating manual entry and the errors that come with it.

Full Gusto setup and ongoing payroll administration

Automated payroll tax filings and year-end W-2 generation

Seamless QuickBooks integration for automatic bookkeeping sync

Employee onboarding, direct deposit, and benefits enrollment support

Simplify Your Payroll
Service 05

Credit Card Merchant Accounts

Accepting credit cards shouldn't require a complicated setup or expensive third-party hardware. Through Intuit's merchant services, we enable card payments directly within your QuickBooks environment.

Speed up your cash flow, modernize how clients pay you, and watch every transaction sync automatically to your books — no manual reconciliation required.

Intuit merchant account setup integrated with your QBO file

Online invoicing with card payment links for faster collections

Automatic transaction matching and reconciliation in QuickBooks

Competitive processing rates and transparent fee structures

Accept Payments Faster
Service 06

Advanced Tax Strategies

Cloud accounting isn't just convenient — it unlocks a level of financial intelligence that traditional bookkeeping never could. Real-time data means forward-looking tax planning, not just last year's numbers.

We work alongside your CPA or tax professional to ensure your books are optimized for tax efficiency all year long — not just at filing time.

Year-round tax positioning, not just year-end scrambling

App integrations that eliminate access issues and data silos

Coordinated reporting for your tax preparer with clean documentation

Proactive advice when we spot opportunities to reduce your tax liability
