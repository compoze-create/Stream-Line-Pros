Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to the questions we hear most often from new clients.

Do I need to be local to Michigan?

Not at all. We're Michigan-based but work with clients nationwide. With cloud accounting tools, location is no barrier to a great working relationship.

Do I need to already use QuickBooks?

No. We can set you up on QuickBooks Online from scratch, migrate you from another system, or optimize a QBO setup you already have.

What if my books are a complete mess?

That's one of the things we do most. Prior year rebuilds are a core service — we'll clean up what's there and set up systems to prevent future problems.

How much do your services cost?

We provide flat-rate, transparent pricing after your free consultation. Every plan is tailored to the specific needs of your business, so we price accordingly.

Will I work directly with Jim and Rebecca?

You will have dedicated team members and direct access to Jim and Rebecca throughout our relationship. You're never handed off to someone who doesn't know your business.

How quickly can you get started?

After the consultation and proposal, most clients are fully onboarded within two to three weeks. For urgent situations, we can often move faster.
